# 🧠 ChainHealthPal

### *Amplifying Your Wellbeing*

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 📌 Overview

**ChainHealthPal** is a Web3-powered AI mental health monitoring
platform that leverages behavioral data such as speech patterns, text
activity, sleep habits, and physical movement to detect early signs of
anxiety, depression, and other mental health conditions.

By combining **Artificial Intelligence (AI)** with **Blockchain
technology (Ethereum)**, ChainHealthPal ensures **privacy, security, and
user ownership of sensitive mental health data** while providing
proactive insights and support.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 🎯 Project Objective

To build a privacy-first, AI-powered Web3 platform that continuously
analyzes behavioral patterns to detect early mental health risks and
provide timely, personalized interventions.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 🚨 Problem Statement

Mental health challenges are increasing globally, yet:

-   Early detection is often missed\
-   Access to professionals is limited\
-   Monitoring is not continuous\
-   Sensitive data is poorly protected\
-   Social stigma discourages seeking help

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 💡 Solution

ChainHealthPal provides:

-   Continuous monitoring of behavioral signals\
-   AI-driven analysis to detect mental health trends\
-   Real-time alerts and insights\
-   Decentralized data storage on Ethereum\
-   Personalized wellness support and resources
